Dr. Jane Maris Sinagub has 20 years of editing experience and specializes in American Psychological Association (APA) formatting. She served as the managing editor for an American Psychological Association journal, associate editor for a Council for Exceptional Children special issue, and has edited numerous academic texts for U.S. and international publishing companies including Allyn and Bacon, Springer, and Sense Publishers. Her editing work for individuals includes faculty at Columbia University (New York), Ewha Womans University (South Korea), National Taiwan Normal University (Taiwan), New York University (New York), University of Hartford (Connecticut), and University of Miami (Florida). She currently provides online editing services to individuals and organizations around the world.
